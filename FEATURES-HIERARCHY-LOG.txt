Hierarchy Notes
- Creator is top override.
- Creator is not restricted by normal permission limits.
- Head Admin and Admin have elevated platform permissions.
- Creator can remove or change higher protected roles.
- Head Admin can manage Admin-level accounts below Creator.
- Admin can manage lower platform roles allowed by the current UI.
- Ministry Head and Ministry Assistant can approve ministry applications for their own ministry.
- Primary Leader is a title only and does not get admin-mode access by default.
