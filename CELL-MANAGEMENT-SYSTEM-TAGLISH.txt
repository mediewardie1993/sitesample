CELL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

1. Main Purpose
Ang Cell Management system ay para matrack ang:
- discipleship growth
- sino ang lead sa kanino
- consolidations
- encounter progress
- promotions and demotions
- cell roster

2. Automatic Discipleship Titles
Ito ang automatic discipleship titles:
- Visitor (Level 1)
- Member (Level 2)
- Cell Leader (Level 3)
- Network Leader (Level 4)

Pwede rin silang i-bestow manually ng:
- Cell Manager
- Admin Pastor
- Senior Pastor

Pwede rin silang mag-promote at mag-demote manually.

3. Leadership Offices
Ito ay hiwalay sa discipleship levels:
- Senior Pastor
- Admin Pastor
- Cell Manager
- Primary Leader

Ang mga office na ito ay manual assignment, hindi automatic.

4. Visitor (Level 1)
Ang Visitor ay may temporary leader na tinatawag na Consolidator.

Rule:
- Ang Consolidator dapat Level 3 pataas
- Ibig sabihin puwedeng:
  - Cell Leader (Level 3)
  - Network Leader (Level 4)
  - o mas mataas na office kapag allowed

Exception:
- Lower levels hindi puwede maging consolidator
- Maliban na lang kung Creator ang mag-assign

Para maging Member (Level 2), kailangan:
- 4 approved consolidations

5. Member (Level 2)
Kapag naka-4 approved consolidations na ang Visitor, automatic o manually approved na puwede siyang maging:
- Member (Level 2)

Kapag Member na, dapat makita:
- Cell Leader
- Cell Group

6. Cell Leader (Level 3)
Para maging Cell Leader, kailangan:
- completed ang Pre-Encounter
- completed ang Encounter
- completed ang Post-Encounter
- successfully consolidated at retained at least 1 visitor under them

Kapag nameet ito, puwedeng:
- automatic title bestow
- o manually i-promote ng Cell Manager pataas

7. Network Leader (Level 4)
Para maging Network Leader, kailangan:
- Cell Leader na siya
- may na-raise siyang at least 2 Cell Leaders
- at ang mga Cell Leaders na iyon ay may hawak nang sarili nilang members

Kapag nameet ito, puwedeng:
- automatic title bestow
- o manually i-promote ng Cell Manager pataas

8. Primary Leader and Network Leader
Magkaiba ang Primary Leader at Network Leader.

Rules:
- Ang Primary Leader ay office/position
- Ang Network Leader ay discipleship title
- Puwede ang isang tao na:
  - Primary Leader lang
  - Network Leader lang
  - parehong Primary Leader at Network Leader

Hindi automatic na pag Primary Leader ay Network Leader na agad.
Hindi rin automatic na pag Network Leader ay Primary Leader na agad.

9. Cell Capacity Rule
Bawat leader/handler can only oversee up to 12 members at a time.

Kasama dito ang mga taong may actual leadership/handling responsibility.

10. Display Priority
Sa Search Profile, priority dapat ang discipleship title.

Suggested order:
1. Leadership office
2. Discipleship title
3. Ministry titles
4. Contact and other profile details

Search should support:
- full name
- username
- discipleship title

11. Roster
Sa fast version, simple roster muna.

Suggested roster:
- grouped by Cell Leader
- puwedeng may Cell Group
- bawat tao may:
  - name
  - discipleship title
  - consolidator or cell leader
  - cell group
  - consolidation count
  - encounter progress

12. Promotion and Demotion
Cell Manager and higher-ups can:
- bestow automatic titles manually
- promote manually
- demote manually

So may balance ang system:
- automatic for normal flow
- manual for exceptions and corrections

13. Transfer Authority
Authorized higher leadership can also:
- transfer visitors to a different consolidator
- transfer members to a different cell leader
- transfer members to a different cell group

So aside from promote and demote, may transfer authority din.

14. Cell Group Dissolvement
Ang Cell Group puwedeng ma-dissolve, pero hindi basta-basta.

Rules:
- Cell Dissolvement requires approval of:
  - Cell Manager
  - and Admin Pastor or Senior Pastor

So hindi puwede isang tao lang ang mag-dissolve.

Bago ma-complete ang dissolvement:
- lahat ng members sa cell group na iyon must be resolved

Options per member:
- ilipat sa another cell group
- or remove as church member

Hindi matatapos ang dissolvement hangga't may unresolved members pa.

15. Fast Build Scope
Unang build muna ng Cell Management should include:
- discipleship titles
- consolidator assignment
- invited by tracking
- invited visitors tracking
- cell leader assignment
- cell group assignment
- consolidation count
- pre-encounter / encounter / post-encounter tracking
- automatic eligibility checks
- manual promote / demote / bestow
- transfer tools
- simple roster

16. Later Upgrade
Future enhancement:
- visual network tree
- highlighted person position in the network
- more advanced reporting
